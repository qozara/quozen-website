The first decentralized expense sharing platform that stores everything in your own Google Drive. No servers, no tracking, just math.

We don't have a database. Your groups, expenses, and settlements are stored as standard Google Sheets in your personal Drive.

AI-Powered Parsing

Natural language processing that works with your own API keys. "I paid $50 for pizza, split with Alice" is all it takes.

Privacy First

Since the app is serverless, your financial data never touches our infrastructure. Total data sovereignty by design.
